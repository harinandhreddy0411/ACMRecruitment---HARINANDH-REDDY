Level 9-10 :

 code: ls
2.Extract the readable strings.
 code: strings data.txt
3.We know the password will look like a 32-character alphanumeric string (similar to previous ones).

 code: strings data.txt | grep -E '[a-zA-Z0-9]{32}'
4.You will get the password as the output
